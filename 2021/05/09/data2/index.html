海洋遥感数据共享II

发表于 2021-05-09 更新于 2024-06-10 阅读次数： Valine：

卫星遥感

  • SST
    https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/data/oceans/pathfinder/Version5.3/L3C/
    也可在GEE获得：

    ee.ImageCollection(“NOAA/CDR/SST_WHOI/V2”)

  • ocean current
    https://podaac-tools.jpl.nasa.gov/drive/files/allData/oscar/L4/oscar_1_deg

  • Sentinel-1/2和Landsat8在线浏览
    https://www.sentinel-hub.com/

  • GNSS和验潮站并置数据
    https://www.sonel.org/?lang=en

  • 高频验潮数据，包含准实时的数据。
    http://www.ioc-sealevelmonitoring.org

  • 英国海洋数据中心，提供CTD、验潮、滑翔机等资料。
    https://www.bodc.ac.uk/

  • 马航370船测地形数据
    http://www.ga.gov.au/about/projects/marine/mh370-data-release

  • 潮汐调和分析T_tide、地图m_map 等Matlab开源程序包
    https://www.eoas.ubc.ca/%7Erich/index.html

  • 中国学者整理的海洋研究领域常用Matlab工具集合
    https://github.com/PanhaiDongPhD/sea-mat/blob/master/src/index.md

  • 欧洲哥白尼海洋数据，含有实测数据、卫星数据等。其中验潮站数据含1min采样。
    https://marine.copernicus.eu/access-data
    帮助：https://notebook.community/pprandi/cmems-training/notebook/01_dataDownload

  • SODA再分析资料，
    https://dsrs.atmos.umd.edu/DATA/soda3.12.2/REGRIDED/ocean/
    https://www.soda.umd.edu/

  • 美国环境信息中心ncei产品，含物理海洋、气候态等（SST，Wind，ssh，Global Temperature and Salinity Profile，）
    https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products

  • World Ocean Atlas
    https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/world-ocean-atlas

  • World Ocean Database
    https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/world-ocean-database

  • NCDC美国，数据会转到NCEI
    https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/

  • NODC，美国。数据会转到NCEI
    https://www.nodc.noaa.gov/access/index.html

  • REMSS公司，数据中心主要有微波传感器观测的参数（wind，SST）
    https://www.remss.com/

  • ESA CCI数据中心（盐度，SST，SSH等）
    https://climate.esa.int/en/odp/#/project/sea-surface-salinity

  • LEGOS，数据涵盖了高度计、温盐、潮汐、重力、水文、冰川等资料。
    https://www.legos.omp.eu/en/homepage/
    https://www.legos.omp.eu/en/data/

  • National Center for Atmospheric Research in Boulder, Colorado. RDA：Cross-Calibrated Multi-Platform Ocean Surface Wind Vector Analysis Product V2, 1987 - ongoing，风场
    https://rda.ucar.edu/datasets/ds745.1/
    contains a large and diverse collection of meteorological and oceanographic observations, operational and reanalysis model outputs, and remote sensing datasets to support atmospheric and geosciences research.

  • ESA数据
    https://earth.esa.int/eogateway/catalog/

  • SSS，盐度
    https://smos-diss.eo.esa.int/oads/access/

  • argo
    http://www.argodatamgt.org/Access-to-data/Argo-GDAC-ftp-and-https-servers
    https://argo.ucsd.edu/data/argo-data-products/

  • CTD：CCHDO (CLIVAR and Carbon Hydrographic Data Office) supports oceanographic research by providing access to high quality, global, vessel-based CTD and hydrographic data from GO-SHIP, WOCE, CLIVAR and other repeat hydrography programs. These data are openly accessible and served in standardized community formats (WHP-Exchange, WOCE, and netCDF). CCHDO also manages public and non-public CTD data for use by the global Argo and OceanSITES programs.
    https://cchdo.ucsd.edu/； https://www.go-ship.org/

  • 盐度数据，含Singularity Analysis Service
    http://bec.icm.csic.es/
